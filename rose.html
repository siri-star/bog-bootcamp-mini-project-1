ROSÉ

Rosé (full name: Roseanne Park) is the main vocalist of BLACKPINK. She was born on February 11, 1997 in Australia. Her solo album "R" has 2 tracks, both of which she helped write. Her unique tambre makes BLACKPINK's songs unbelievably addicting - check out some of my favorite covers of hers below!

Fun fact: Prior to debuting with BLACKPINK, she collaborated with G-Dragon on his 2012 track "Without You", which peaked at #10 on the Gaon Music Chart.
