JISOO

Jisoo (full name: Kim Jisoo) is the visual member of BLACKPINK. Jisoo is the oldest member of BLACKPINK born on January 3, 1995 in South Korea. In addition to her beauty, Jisoo is the most witty member of BLACKPINK, so no matter where she goes, she is always super fun to watch! Jisoo has starred in many commercials and most recently, a hit K-drama called "Snowdrop" (check out the trailer below!).
